My life outside the classroom

Challanges shape our charachter, caste, our attitude and instate the basic principle of life. I love accepting challanges, whether it be possible or impossible to complete. The possible challanges for me referred to the challanges that I got in sports.

I have enjoyed playing sports since my childhood, especially Cricket. When I was in seventh standard, my aim was to become a cricketer but after sometime I realised the competition and kept my passion limited to it. Even now, whenever I get a chance to play Cricket, I grab the oppurtunity. Now, let me introduce to to another sport that has become a part of my daily routine. During the lockdown period of 2020, when everyone was concerned about their health, I got inspired by a TV program named 'Corona se jung, Swami Ramdev ke sang' and started following it. Being a beginner it felt difficult for a couple of weeks but eventually I started feeling relaxed, fit and calm. Yoga is something that I would definitely recommend all the readers to start practicing.

One more thing that I would like to add on is videogames. I like playing videogames(RP) in my free time. I have also participated in several inter and intra college tournaments and have won prizes.
